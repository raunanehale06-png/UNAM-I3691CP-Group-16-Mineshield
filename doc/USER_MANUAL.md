## Introduction
MineShield is a safety app for mining environments. It provides real-time hazard reporting, fall detection, and emergency alerts.

## Installation

## Worker Guide
### Logging In
- Use credentials provided by your supervisor
- Dashboard shows your recent hazards and quick actions

### Reporting a Hazard
1. Tap "Report Hazard"
2. Enter description and select zone
3. Choose severity (Low/Medium/High)
4. Take a photo (optional)
5. Submit – supervisors see it instantly

### Fall Detection
- The app detects sudden impacts
- A confirmation modal appears – respond within 10 seconds
- If no response, SOS is sent automatically

### SOS Button
- Tap the red SOS button for emergencies
- Your last known location is shared with all supervisors

## Supervisor Guide
### Live Map
- View all active hazards as colored markers
- Green = resolved, Red = pending, Yellow = in progress

### Analytics Dashboard
- Safety score, hazard trends, and resolution times
- Filter by date range (24h, 7d, 30d)

### Personnel Tracking
- See all workers and their last known location
- Status badges: On-Duty, Lost Signal, Off-Duty

## Visitor Guide
- Read-only access
- View safe zones and active alerts
- Cannot report hazards

## Troubleshooting
- No GPS underground? → App uses last known location
- No internet? → Offline mode buffers reports
- Fall detection too sensitive? → Contact support
